What I Care About

 
I am student at Virginia Tech studying Computer Engineering.

My open mind allows me to seek truth in the universe and appreciate the wonderful life I have.

Actively, I do my best to care for the environment and spread my passion for space exploration!

If we keep polluting our atmosphere without regulation, our climate could become simliar to the climate on Venus, where a runaway greenhouse effect (mainly caused by CO2) could heat the planet to uninhabitable temperatures.

Home

 
Home for me is an undistinguishable corner of our Pale Blue Dot, the Earth.

I grew up in Central Pennsylvania and decided to study engineering at Virginia Tech in Blacksburg, VA.

Most of my younger life consisted of going to my family's cabin near Raystown Lake in PA, playing Lacrosse for my high school and various clubs, and programming simple computer games with Java and C++.

Future Goals
