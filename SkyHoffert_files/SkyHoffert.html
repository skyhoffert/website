What I Care About

 
I am student at Virginia Tech studying Computer Engineering.

My open mind allows me to seek truth in the universe and appreciate the wonderful life I have.

Actively, I do my best to care for the environment and spread my passion for space exploration!

If we keep polluting our atmosphere without regulation, our climate could become simliar to the climate on Venus, where a runaway greenhouse effect (mainly caused by CO2) could heat the planet to uninhabitable temperatures.

Home

 
Home for me is an undistinguishable corner of our Pale Blue Dot, the Earth.

I grew up in Central Pennsylvania and decided to study engineering at Virginia Tech in Blacksburg, VA.

Most of my younger life consisted of going to my family's cabin near Raystown Lake in PA, playing Lacrosse for my high school and various clubs, and programming simple computer games with Java and C++.

Future Goals

 
As a species, we have accomplished so much in just a short amount of time.

We are also stuck in the past, killing other humans just to show we have power.

I try my best to advocate for science and space exploration. Every day, I look up and wonder what it would be like to explore some new place in the Solar System or beyond.

Who knows, maybe my future will lead me to a place like Mars!


In high school I worked for my Father's company H&M Diversified. I had a large variety of experience here. I cut glass, assembled metal frames, installed glass/frames into commercial buildings, helped install shower enclosures in residential buildings, transported supplies with large vehicles, approved invoices, checked CAD drawings, and measured with project managers. This job gave me a huge variety of general knowledge and skills with tools that I would not have gotten otherwise.
 


During my sophomore year at Virginia Tech, I decided to take a co-op position at Solers. There, I did a lot of bleeding edge work with popular software such as Amazon AWS for cloud-computing, Kubernetes/docker container management, automation software such as Puppet, building tools like Maven, and cross server database storage such as Splunk.


My dream is that this experience will make me an outstanding candidate for a job dealing with space science. One way or another, I will find a way to help humanity reach its arms towards space!
